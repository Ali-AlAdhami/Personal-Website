Project Excerpt

Feast for Friends is a tool for those who are looking for recipes, figuring out what they can cook with a certain ingredient, or want to find a restaurant. The website features recipes, ingredients, and restaurants from a variety of cultures. Feast for Friends helps you find great recipes that fit any appetite or budget. Have an ingredient that you want to incorporate in your cooking? Feast for Friends gives you recipes that use what you have. Not in the mood for cooking? Feast for Friends will provide restaurants to fit your tastes.
